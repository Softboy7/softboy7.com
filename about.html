About

Hey, I'm Softboy7.

If you want to know more about me and the lore of my music,
this is the right place.

I love creating.

Making something is the best. Like even drawing a stick figure with a cool hat. You made something! Just like

HOW COOL IS THAT

It didn't exist but then you had an idea in your head and brought it into the real/digital world.

When it comes to music and everything around it, it's the same thing, just more complex. I take inspiration from very emotional real life situations and merge them with ultra specific aesthetics that I'm obsessed with. All of my albums follow storylines and visions that I create from scratch.

Of course, my music is for everyone who wants to listen, but I strongly sympathize with outcasts and lonely people because I think the feeling of not fitting in is so relatable. Being different is not a bad thing, tho. It means you held onto something that many lose throughout life: your curiosity, your fantasy-like imagination, your courage to be yourself.
I think that's something to be proud of.

With all that being said... I ain't no company or businessman who chases mass appeal. I'm just a guy, independent, DIY asf, often in his pajamas converting feelings into music.
I'm really grateful I get to do that, fr ♡

LORE:
The "welcome to the void" lore is up on my Discord server but I don't recommend reading it if you're under 16.
As for the dreamData lore, I want to wait before publishing it. I want to give people the chance to connect their own feelings and stories to it. All I can say is: it's a very personal project, and I’m super proud it’s finally coming out. I worked on it for over 1.5 years.
